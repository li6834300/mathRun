# Privacy Policy for Arrow Rush

**Effective Date**: December 8, 2024

## Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Arrow Rush mobile application ("App") for iOS devices.

## Information Collection and Use

Arrow Rush does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information or user data.

### What We Don't Collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
- Location data
- Device information
- Usage analytics
- Advertising data
- Third-party tracking

### Game Center Integration

Arrow Rush integrates with Apple's Game Center for leaderboard functionality. Game Center is operated by Apple Inc. and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your Game Center data.

When you use Game Center features:
- Your Game Center username and scores are handled by Apple
- We do not collect or store this information on our servers
- Please review Apple's Game Center Privacy Policy for details

### Data Storage

The App stores your high score locally on your device using iOS secure storage. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any servers
- Is not shared with third parties
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
